Latitude v. Humidity

Our graph here shows no traditional correlation, but there are still some patterns to note. Notably, there appear to be outliers around a latitude of positive 60 and a humidity of 0%. These may either be fantastic cities to live in if you don't like a humid climate or there was some issue with collecting the data correctly. All that to say, it's something to investigate. Other questions that seem to arise from the data: if you split the graph by 60% humidity, do the cities that are grouped below that share other features, such as their climate or general region of the world? Do the cities that sit on the line of 100% humidity also share commonalities, even though they span latitudes that put them all over the globe?
