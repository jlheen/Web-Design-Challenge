Latitude v. Cloudiness

Cloudiness certainly does not have a graph appropriate for a linear regression model. That does not mean there are no patterns present. There appear to be three clusters of city groups that sit along a certain line of cloudiness percentage: a group sitting on 0%, a group sitting a little under 80% and a group hovering around 90%. What's even more interesting is that since these cities span latitudes of almost -60 to positive 80, though they share this cloudiness percentage they are likely located all over the globe. It would be very interesting to study where these cities are located and what other relationships exist (or do not exist!) among the cities and their regions.
